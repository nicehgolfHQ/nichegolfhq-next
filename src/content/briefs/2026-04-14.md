- [U.S. Mid-Amateur Entry Opens April 22 at Sand Valley Resort](https://championships.usga.org/usmidamateur.html) — USGA
  On 2026-04-14: The USGA has announced that entries for the 2026 U.S. Mid-Amateur Championship open on April 22, with the championship set for September 26 through October 1 at Sand Valley Resort (The Lido) in Nekoosa, Wisconsin. Competitors must be at least 25 years old and carry a Handicap Index of 2.4 or lower. Entry closes July 22.

  On 2026-04-11: Bryan Hoops of Tempe, Arizona closed with a 3-under 69 to win the 2026 Senior Masters at Indian Wells Golf Resort by one shot over Rick Stimmel, finishing at 7-under 208 for the tournament. It marks Hoops' second consecutive Senior Masters title in the Society of Seniors' marquee spring event.
