  On 2026-04-11: Brandon Holtz, the 39-year-old real estate agent from Illinois who won the 2025 U.S. Mid-Amateur at Troon Country Club, saw his Masters week come to an end after missing the cut at Augusta National. His journey from reinstated amateur to Mid-Am champion to Masters competitor is the kind of story that defines the mid-am game.

- [AJGA Juniors Shine at Augusta National Women’s Amateur Despite Talley’s Final-Round Collapse](https://www.ajga.org/news/four-ajga-juniors-shine-on-road-to-anwa-final-round) — AJGA
  On 2026-04-11: Four AJGA juniors made the final-round cut at the 2026 Augusta National Women’s Amateur, headlined by 17-year-old Asterisk Talley who held an 11-under lead through 36 holes. Talley set the ANWA record for consecutive bogey-free holes at 36 before a quadruple-bogey on the iconic 12th hole Saturday derailed her title bid. Colombia’s Maria Jose Marin ultimately claimed the championship at 14-under, while Talley finished tied for fourth.
