- [U.S. Mid-Amateur Champion Brandon Holtz Lives Out His Masters Dream at Augusta](https://thespun.com/golf/brandon-holtz-is-the-feel-good-story-of-the-2026-masters) — The Spun
  On 2026-04-10: Brandon Holtz, the 39-year-old Bloomington, Illinois real estate agent who won the 2025 U.S. Mid-Amateur at Troon Country Club, teed it up in the first round of the Masters on Thursday. - [Maria Jose Marin Wins Augusta National Women's Amateur as AJGA Juniors Shine](https://www.ajga.org/news/four-ajga-juniors-shine-on-road-to-anwa-final-round) — AJGA
  On 2026-04-10: Colombia's Maria Jose Marin captured the 2026 Augusta National Women's Amateur title with a final-round 68, finishing at 14-under par. Four AJGA juniors made the cut, led by top-ranked Asterisk Talley of Chowchilla, California, who held the 36-hole lead at 11-under before a quadruple bogey on the iconic No. 12 hole derailed her bid.
